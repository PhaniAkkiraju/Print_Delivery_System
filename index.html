Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your personal data

1. Information We Collect

We collect details you provide directly, such as your username, email address, password hash, phone number, delivery addresses, and uploaded PDF print jobs. File uploads are temporarily stored to complete your print and delivery process.

2. Document Protection and Retention

Your uploaded document security is our top priority. PDF documents are stored in secure, access-restricted repositories. Once printing is completed and order delivery is finalized, files are automatically purged from our servers within 24 hours.

3. Real-Time Tracking Data

To enable live 15-minute scooter delivery, we track order dispatch timing and delivery route metrics. No persistent geolocation profiles are kept after the transaction has finished.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about PrintIt

⏱️ How does the 15-minute delivery work? ▼
We operate local print micro-hubs in key city zones. Once your PDF order is placed, printing and automated packing takes less than 3 minutes, and the nearest delivery partner receives and delivers it within the remaining time.
📄 What file formats are supported? ▼
Currently, we exclusively accept PDF files (.pdf) to maintain print layout formatting, pagination accuracy, and instant automated page counting.
🌀 What binding options do you provide? ▼
We offer soft Spiral Binding (+₹40) with transparent front covers and hard Book Binding (+₹60) for professional reports, presentations, and booklets.

About PrintIt

The story of how we made print runs instant

⚡🖨️🛵

Reinventing Paper Logistics

PrintIt was founded in 2026 to solve a simple problem: running out of physical copies when heading to an interview, lecture, or meeting is extremely stressful. Traditional copy stores require long waits, file transfers, and tedious queues.

By building local micro-centers equipped with automated high-speed printer networks, we deliver physical printouts straight to your coordinates in under 15 minutes. Pure velocity, premium prints, zero stress.
